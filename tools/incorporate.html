Incorporating Medication into Daily Routines

Tips to help make your medication regimen part of your everyday life.

Tips for Adherence

  • Link to Existing Habits: Take your medication while doing a regular activity, like brushing your teeth in the morning.
  • Use Visual Cues: Place your medications in visible spots (e.g., next to your coffee maker) as reminders.
  • Set Reminders: Use alarms or apps as additional reminders to help you stay consistent.
  • Establish a Routine: Make medication adherence a fixed part of your daily routine, such as taking meds right after meals.
  • Keep a Journal: Track your medication intake and how you feel to stay motivated and monitor any side effects.
