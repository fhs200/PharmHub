Medication Reminder Systems

Set reminders and integrate them with your calendar to stay on top of your medications.

Set Up Medication Reminders

Use one of the following options to set up your medication reminders:

  • Calendar Integration: Sync your medication schedule with Google Calendar, iCal, or other apps to get reminders on your devices.
  • SMS Reminders: Receive text message reminders at specific times to take your medication.
  • App Notifications: Use our mobile app to set recurring notifications for each medication.
